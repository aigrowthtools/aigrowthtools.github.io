The Keyword Magic Tool contains over 25 billion keywords across 142 geographic databases — including dedicated databases for India, USA, UK, Canada, Australia, Germany, and virtually every major market globally.

### 🔧 Feature 3: Site Audit — Find Every Technical Issue Hurting Your Rankings
Semrush crawls your entire website and produces a prioritized list of every technical SEO issue that may be suppressing your search performance. Many bloggers see immediate ranking improvements simply by fixing the issues Semrush surfaces — without writing a single new word.

The Site Audit checks over 140 technical SEO parameters, including:
* Broken internal and external links (404 errors hurting crawl efficiency)
* Page speed and Core Web Vitals issues affecting Google rankings
* Missing or duplicate meta descriptions reducing click-through rates
* Duplicate content confusing Google about which page to rank
* Missing image alt text hurting accessibility and image SEO
* Crawlability issues — pages Google cannot properly index
* HTTPS and SSL problems reducing trust signals
* Thin content — pages with insufficient content to rank competitively
* Internal linking gaps — pages that need stronger link support

Each issue is categorized as Error, Warning, or Notice — so you fix the most impactful problems first rather than wasting time on minor optimizations.
