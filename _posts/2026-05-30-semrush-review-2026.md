## Key Features: What SEMrush Actually Does

### 🔍 Feature #1: Keyword Magic Tool — 25 Billion+ Keywords
This is Semrush's flagship feature and the one that directly determines whether your blog generates organic traffic.

The Keyword Magic Tool contains over 25 billion keywords across 142 geographic databases — including India, UK, USA, Canada, Australia, and virtually every major market worldwide.

Enter any seed keyword — say, "web hosting" — and the tool returns:
* **Monthly search volume** — how many people search this term per month
* **Keyword Difficulty (KD%)** — how hard it is to rank on page 1 (0–100 scale)
* **CPC (Cost Per Click)** — what advertisers pay, which signals commercial value
* **Search Intent** — Informational, Commercial, Transactional, or Navigational

![SEMrush Keyword Magic Tool](/assets/img/posts/semrush-keyword-magic.jpg)

The Search Intent filter is the single most valuable feature for affiliate bloggers. Most bloggers waste enormous effort writing informational content (*"What is web hosting?"*) when the money is in commercial and transactional keywords (*"best web hosting for bloggers 2026"*). Semrush's intent filter lets you instantly separate buyer-intent keywords from browser-intent keywords.

Additional Keyword Tools included:
* **Keyword Overview** — deep analysis of any single keyword
* **Keyword Gap** — compare your keyword profile against competitors and find terms they rank for that you don't
* **Organic Research** — see every keyword any website ranks for (including competitors)

### 🕵️ Feature #2: Competitor Analysis — Your Competitors' Playbook, Exposed
This feature alone justifies the Semrush subscription for affiliate marketers.

**Organic Research:** Type any competitor's domain and see their top-performing pages by traffic, estimated monthly organic visits, and their top referring domains. This enables a powerful workflow: find a site monetizing the same affiliate programs you promote, identify their top 10 traffic pages, analyze their keywords, and create better, more comprehensive versions.

**Keyword Gap Analysis:** Upload your domain and 1–4 competitor domains. Semrush shows you keywords your competitors rank for that you don't target at all — instant content opportunities that you know have proven search demand.

### 🔧 Feature #3: Site Audit — Find Every Technical Issue Hurting Your Rankings
SEMrush crawls your entire website and produces a prioritized list of every technical SEO issue affecting your search performance:
* **Broken links (404 errors)** — hurting both user experience and crawl efficiency
* **Slow page load times** — directly affecting Core Web Vitals and rankings
* **Missing meta descriptions** — reducing click-through rates in search results
* **Duplicate content / Crawlability issues** — pages Google can't properly index

Each issue is categorized as Error, Warning, or Notice — so you fix the most damaging problems first. Running a Site Audit is the first thing I do when evaluating any blog — before any keyword research or content planning. You can't build on a broken foundation.

### 📊 Feature #4: Position Tracking — Watch Your Rankings Move Daily
Set up a project for your website, enter your target keywords (up to 500 on Pro plan), and Semrush tracks your daily ranking position for each keyword — on both desktop and mobile, in any country.

What Position Tracking shows you:
* Your current ranking for each keyword (position 1–100)
* Daily ranking changes — up arrows (improving) and down arrows (declining)
* SERP Feature visibility — are you appearing in Featured Snippets or People Also Ask?

In my testing, I tracked 200 keywords across my test blog for 30 days. Position Tracking revealed 12 keywords hovering between positions 8–15 — the "almost there" zone. I updated those posts using Semrush's recommendations, and 7 of the 12 moved to page 1 within 3 weeks.
