## What You Can Contact Us About

### 💬 General Feedback
Loved an article? Found it helpful? Think something could be improved or explained better? We welcome all feedback — it genuinely helps us create better content.

### 🔍 Tool Review Requests
Is there an AI tool you would like us to review that we have not covered yet? Let us know! We consider every suggestion seriously and add worthwhile tools to our content calendar.

### ⚠️ Content Corrections
If you have found a factual error, outdated pricing, or incorrect information in any of our articles, please tell us right away. Accuracy is a core commitment for us and we correct errors as quickly as possible.

### 🤝 Collaboration and Partnerships
Are you a tool company, SaaS brand, or fellow blogger interested in a genuine collaboration? Send us an email with the details of what you have in mind. We are open to partnerships that provide real value to our readers.

### 📝 Guest Post Inquiries
We accept high-quality guest posts on topics including AI tools, blogging, SEO, content creation, and digital marketing. Guest posts must be:

- **100% original** — not published elsewhere
- **Well-researched** and accurate
- **Genuinely useful** to bloggers and digital creators
- **At least 1,500 words** with proper structure

Please email us with your proposed topic and a brief outline before writing the full article.

### 🔗 Affiliate and Sponsorship Inquiries
If you are reaching out about a sponsored post, paid review, or affiliate collaboration, please clearly state this in the subject line of your email. We review all proposals individually.

Please note: we only promote tools and services we believe are genuinely valuable to our readers. We do not publish sponsored content that conflicts with our editorial standards.

### 📋 DMCA and Copyright Issues
If you believe content on our website infringes your copyright, please refer to our [Terms and Conditions](/terms-and-conditions/) for the full DMCA notice procedure, then send your notice to **karnaray790@gmail.com**.

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Q: Do you accept paid reviews or sponsored content?**
We consider sponsorship proposals on a case-by-case basis. However, we never allow payment to influence the editorial tone or conclusions of a review. Any sponsored content is clearly labeled as such.

**Q: Do you respond to every email?**
Yes — we respond to every email personally. If you have not received a reply within 48 business hours, please check your spam folder and try again.

**Q: Can I republish your articles on my website?**
No. All content on AI Growth Tools is protected by copyright. You may quote brief excerpts (up to 50 words) with proper credit and a link back to the original article. Full republication requires prior written permission. See our [Terms and Conditions](/terms-and-conditions/) for details.

**Q: Do you offer SEO or blogging consulting services?**
We do not currently offer consulting services. All of our guidance is published freely on the blog.
